PORTFOLIO ABOUT RESUME
CARNEGIE MELLON INFOGRAPHIC


During my free time I really enjoy graphic designing. In the summer of 2013 I interned at a startup in San Francisco and fell in love with the city. This inspired me to create a graphic representation that highlighted some of the well-known features of the area. I decided to include the Golden Gate Bridge, the cityscape, and the hills which I felt contributed to my experience in the city.
